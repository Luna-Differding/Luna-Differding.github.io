3D Print- Muscle Man

Statement

Aside from the significance Regular Show had on me and my generation, I find it's creator, J.G. Quintel, to be one of my inspirations. In an interview he did with Hot Topic, he expressed that since he was little all he ever wanted to do was draw. I feel that as Artists, many people expect us to get "serious" jobs. But for me, Quintel, and really all artists, it's important that we stick with our passions, refine our talents, and share them with the world as a means of communicating the human experience in meaningful ways. I 3D printed my Minecraft build, and it serves now as an homage to Quintel, and as a reminder to myself that if I work hard, I can be successful while doing what I love.

Process

Process: I captured the build I made of Muscle Man using mineways, and was able to save the image as an stl file, which I was able to 3D print.
