Process

My first ever attempt at creating a coherent image using Java Script, this selfie was constructed using a series of vertexes and colors I used to fill the completed shapes. It challenged me to think of the image in terms of x and y axes, and I used a notebook paper to help me visualize where points might end up. It was a lot of guessing and checking.
