Net Art- Elixir

Statement

As a creative, I have explored many outlets of self expression, one of which being creative writing. I love telling stories for the same reason I love making art, and that is the precious opportunity to transport the reader or viewer to another reality or place in time. World building is one of my favorite components of creating. and this project was the perfect chance to combine images and color pallets with an interactive written storyline, which offers the "player" an opportunity to choose their own paths and explore the imaginary realm I created.

Process

I coded this net art website using HTML. The player is able to experience the story first hand through clickable links, which guide the player to unique places within the storyline. By inserting the player into the story as the main character, they are able to engage more intimately with the story and really feel the emotions it invokes.
