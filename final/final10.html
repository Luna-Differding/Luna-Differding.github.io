PHENOMENAL INTENTIONALITY

A DISEMBODIED EXPERIENCE

Life is typically out of our control. We will miss out on the wonders of the world if we are not present, spontaneous, and wholly ourselves. We do a disservice to those we love if we diminish the essence of our personalities, and spend hours toiling over our behavior. The time we spend wrapped up in ourselves is time taken away from those we hold most dear.
