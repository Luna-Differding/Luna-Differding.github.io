Glitch Art- Chronology

Statement: Chronology is a work which conveys the origins of humanity and it's developments over time. The work functions as a series or the final compilation of images. Through gradual addition of color, Chronology suggests that we slowly developed the characteristics that make us human. We were once nothing. We then existed simply and animalistically--our purpose to survive and reproduce. Finally, eons later, we are able to exist in a reality were we can indulge ourselves and see the world in technicolor.
