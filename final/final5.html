PHENOMENAL INTENTIONALITY

A DISEMBODIED EXPERIENCE

The loathsome combined with the admirable qualities we possess are inseparable. They are what makes us who we are. Using the phenomenal intentional approach asks us to leave our complexities at the door and learn to reflect on our failures and biases and reform ourselves. To act neutrally in order to live more harmoniously.

What do you value more? Getting along well with others? Or the freedom to unabashedly express yourself?
