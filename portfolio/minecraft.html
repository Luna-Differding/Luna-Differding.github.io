Minecraft Pixel Art- Muscle Man

Statement

My rendition of Muscle Man from the popular animated series "Regular Show" symbolizes the origins of my appreciation for digital artworks. The show goes far beyond what many children's shows accomplish. Contrary to what the title suggests, the show follows the outrageous, off-beat adventures of it's characters. There is a certain cultural significance it provided for kids in the late 2000's, as it taught us that normalcy is monotonous. It encouraged us to find humor and amusement in the oddities which present themselves in our lives; to embrace them and let loose. The show was instrumental to my development as well as the development of countless young people across the globe. See how I converted this to a 3D print HERE.
