PHENOMENAL INTENTIONALITY

A DISEMBODIED EXPERIENCE

Using the webcam, look at yourself closely. Think of all the traits you possess that you find to be admirable. Think about the last social interaction you had where you made someone feel good about themselves. It could be as simple as smiling at strangers or listening to someone’s troubles. Think about the things that make you unique: your innermost desires, your passions, good things you’ve done when no one is watching.
